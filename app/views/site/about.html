Zambia Hash

All The Latest News from Zambia in One Place

On July 1, 2013, Google decided to retire one of its popular products called Google Reader, a news aggregator created in early 2005, stating the product had a loyal but declining following, and they wanted to focus on fewer products (see https://www.google.com/reader/about/)

Zambia Hash is a response to that announcement to provide an alternative for a niche Zambian audience.

Zambia Hash is the one place where you can read all the latest news headlines from all the major newspaper websites from Zambia.

Zambia Hash allows you see when websites have added new content. You can get the latest headlines from all the major online news websites in one place as soon as it is published.

Zambia Hash just gives you the except of all the stories published and you can always visit the original site if you need to read the full story.
